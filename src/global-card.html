International card payments are easy.

Just connect to our platform via a single API and you’re good to go!

Doesn’t matter if you have online business or it’s a coffee shop in your home town, it’s essential to accept credit and debit cards from your customers.

Nowadays over half of all global online transactions are made by card. With our global acquiring network, you can process your customer’s card payments easily around the world.

Why work with a global acquirer like us?

Partnering with us lets you shortcut your way to a complete payments setup. Getting card payments up and running can be a complicated task, especially if you’re managing multiple areas. That’s where we come in.

After all, you shouldn’t have to spend your valuable time managing accounts just to process a customer’s transaction. With our help, you’ve got an ideal partner to handle all that technical boring stuff.

  • Expand your business with cross-border payments

    Easily scale up internationally when you rely on our global acquiring network that’s present in 170 countries, with 150 currencies supported.

  • One vendor for your entire payment solution

    We work with you to design a cost-effective and efficient payments setup, with consolidated reporting and big data analysis at hand whenever you need.

  • Backed by our partners

    With our partners lending and working capital solutions, we’ve got an even stronger platform and a worldwide distribution network.

Advantages of our worldwide payment solution

As a payment provider, we take care of all your payment needs — across markets, currencies and channels for better approval ratios throughout the board. Our platform is designed to support your business around the world, giving you all the time in the world to raise your revenue. Check out the benefits you can look forward to when you work with us.

  • Enjoy higher authorization rates

    With our local processing and fraud checks, you can connect to global and regional card schemes worldwide — improving your revenue and reducing time to market in Europe, the US, Asia and beyond.

  • Keep interchange fees low

    Say hello to savings on interchange and currency exchange fees with favorable local market conditions on your cross-border sales.

  • Rely on our compliance and regulation experts

    Our experts have got you covered when it comes to local taxes, laws and the latest scheme updates from Visa, Mastercard and other payment systems.

  • Rest easy with like-for-like settlements

    Take advantage of lower currency conversion rates and settlement costs when you sell and pay out in your customer‘s local currency.

  • A payment gateway that’s built for your growth

    What is a gateway anyway, you might ask? In a nutshell, it’s how we connect your customers’ payment request to your business.

    Our gateway is PCI-DSS compliant, supports 3D Secure 2.0 and offers 80+ alternative payment methods. All you need is a single API and you’ve got access to an entire range of services. Now you can accept payments from any device, anywhere in the world.

  • Give your customers the payment options they’re looking for

    People are always more comfortable paying in their own preferred currency. We get it. That’s why with us you can accept the world’s most popular payment methods for better conversion in multiple markets. Take payments from Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, and the local favorites too — in over 170 countries.

    Oh, did we mention with us that’s possible in over 150 transaction currencies? Pretty hard to beat.
